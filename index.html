Divino Niño is an American rock band formed in Chicago, Illinois in 2012. The band consists of Camilo Medina (vox/guitar), Javier Forero (vox/bass), Guillermo Rodriguez (vox/guitars), and Pierce Codina (cosmic vibes & interstellar drums). They are known for their psychedelic sound, drum loops, harmonies, world percussion, and Latin American roots.
